# Whiskly Baker Lifecycle Communications

All communications aligned with the Phase 1 model decisions:
- 0% commission, $4.99 customer fee, no monthly fee during Phase 1
- Commission activates at 40% Whiskly-sourced (90-day rolling) OR 18 months
- Three tiers when commission activates: Free 10%, Pro $19/mo + 7%, Elite $34/mo + 5%
- 60 days written notice before commission activates
- Founding Baker benefits: permanent featured placement, locked-in tier pricing, 12 months free Pro/Elite

## 2. Onboarding Disclosure Screen (in-app, after first login, must be acknowledged)

This is shown the first time a baker logs in after approval, before they can create a listing. Requires checkbox acknowledgment to proceed.

> ### Welcome to Whiskly. Before you start, here's what to know.
>
> We want every baker on Whiskly to feel informed before listing their first item. Please read and acknowledge before continuing.
>
> #### What you'll pay right now
>
> - $0 to join Whiskly
> - $0/month
> - **0% commission on every order**
> - You receive 100% of your listed order price (less standard Stripe processing fees)
> - Your customer pays a $4.99 platform fee at checkout, separate from your price
>
> #### What changes in the future
>
> Commission will activate when one of two things happens, whichever comes first:
>
> 1. At least **40% of your orders over a 90-day period** come from customers Whiskly brought to you (we call this "Whiskly-sourced"), OR
> 2. **18 months from today** ([calculated activation deadline date])
>
> When commission activates, you'll choose between three tiers:
>
> | Tier | Monthly Fee | Commission |
> |---|---|---|
> | Free | $0 | 10% per order |
> | Pro | $19/month | 7% per order |
> | Elite | $34/month | 5% per order |
>
> You'll get **60 days written notice** before commission activates for you. No surprises.
>
> #### What "Whiskly-sourced" means
>
> An order is Whiskly-sourced if both of these are true:
>
> - The customer found you through Whiskly's browse, search, or recommendations (not your own Instagram link or external marketing)
> - The customer hadn't ordered from you on Whiskly before
>
> Customers who came to your profile via your own marketing (Instagram, Facebook, your existing customer base) are NOT counted as Whiskly-sourced. Those orders never trigger commission.
>
> #### What this means in plain English
>
> We don't take a cut of orders you'd have gotten anyway. We only earn commission when we're actually bringing you new customers. You can track your Whiskly-sourced percentage in your baker dashboard at any time.
>
> #### If you're a Founding Baker
>
> Whether or not you knew it when you applied, the first 10 bakers on Whiskly are Founding Bakers. You get:
>
> - Permanent featured placement in browse results
> - Locked-in tier pricing for the lifetime of your account ($19/mo Pro, $34/mo Elite, even if we raise prices later)
> - 12 months free Pro or Elite tier when commission activates
> - Direct input on platform features through monthly founder calls
>
> #### Got questions?
>
> Email Lxy directly at [founder email]. Real human, fast reply.
>
> ☐ **I've read and understand how Whiskly's pricing works.**
>
> [Continue to your dashboard]

## 8. Implementation notes for Claude Code

**Email infrastructure:**
- All emails sent through Resend (per current platform setup)
- Use baker's display name in greeting; fall back to first name from full name field
- Link to dashboard should be a magic-link login if possible (no password re-entry)
- All emails should have a footer link to email preferences (so bakers can opt out of non-essential ones)
- Never let bakers opt out of the 60-day commission activation notice or any tier-selection reminder. These are operationally required.

**Onboarding disclosure screen:**
- Block first dashboard access until acknowledged
- Save acknowledgment to database with timestamp and version number (in case disclosure text changes later)
- Recommended: store a hash of the disclosure text the baker acknowledged, so you can prove what they agreed to if ever disputed

**Monthly impact report:**
- Send via Resend on the 1st of every month at 9 AM ET
- Calculate metrics for previous calendar month
- Use cron job (Vercel Pro required for hourly precision)
- Skip if baker joined within last 14 days (no meaningful data yet); send first report on the 1st of the month after a full month on the platform

**Threshold-approaching notice:**
- Triggered automatically when baker's rolling 90-day Whiskly-sourced % crosses 30%
- Send only once per account (don't re-send if % drops back below 30% then crosses again)
- Skip if baker is within 60 days of 18-month backstop (the formal notice will cover them anyway)

**60-day commission activation notice:**
- Triggered automatically when baker crosses 40% rolling OR hits 18-month mark
- Set activation_date field to (today + 60 days)
- Lock in trigger reason at this moment (40% vs 18-month) for audit trail
- This email kicks off the reminder sequence (30-day and 7-day reminders auto-scheduled)

**Tier defaults:**
- If baker doesn't select tier by activation date, default to Free
- Send default-applied confirmation email automatically
- Founding Baker 12-month free benefit applies ONLY if Pro/Elite was selected during the 60-day activation window. After that window closes, the benefit is forfeited.

**Database fields needed:**
- `baker.disclosure_acknowledged_at` (timestamp)
- `baker.disclosure_version_acknowledged` (string, e.g., "v1.0")
- `baker.commission_activation_date` (date, null until triggered)
- `baker.commission_activation_trigger` (enum: '40_percent', '18_month_backstop', 'early_activation', null)
- `baker.tier_selected` (enum: 'free', 'pro', 'elite', null)
- `baker.tier_selected_at` (timestamp)
- `baker.is_founding_baker` (boolean)
- `baker.founding_baker_free_tier_expires_at` (date, only set if Founding Baker on Pro/Elite)
- `baker.threshold_30_notice_sent_at` (timestamp, null until sent once)

**Edge cases to handle:**
- Baker pauses account during activation window: pause the 60-day countdown
- Baker disputes the Whiskly-sourced calculation: provide a clear path in the activation email to "request a review" that pauses activation pending manual review
- Baker accepts Founding Baker benefits in onboarding but later the count exceeds 10 (your manual review backlog might cause this): honor the offer for everyone who saw the disclosure with Founding Baker language at acknowledgment time, even if it pushes your Founding Baker count above 10. Trust matters more than the cap.

## 9. What to ship first

If you can only build a subset right now, ship in this order:

1. **Onboarding disclosure screen** (Section 2) - blocks new baker activation, must exist before next baker is approved
2. **Welcome email** (Section 1) - sent immediately on approval, easy to template
3. **Monthly impact report** (Section 3) - first send isn't until the 1st of next month, so you have time
4. **All other communications** (Sections 4 to 7) - not triggered until much later in baker lifecycle (months from now). Build before then but not blocking launch.

Items 1 and 2 are launch-blocking. Item 3 should ship within 30 days. Items 4 to 7 can ship within 90 days.
